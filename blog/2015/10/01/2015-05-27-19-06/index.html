Creativity is a fascinating subject, it is the beauty and challenge of mathematics at the same time. When I was in Germany, I took part in a project about creativity. The goal was to write a book discussing this topic from the perspective of all kinds of subjects. There were articles about creativity and biology, creativity and law, creativity and religion, creativity and music ..., and I also wrote something about creativity and mathematics. Moreover, we interviewed scientists and artists about their opinion. It was interesting to compare the roles creativity plays, and we realized that although the subjects are very different, there are common features. For instance that it's mysterious how we get ideas, they seem to come out of nowhere. But really, thinking about it, we realize that there is a long process of preparation (sometimes unconscious); that's what you describe as hard work.
