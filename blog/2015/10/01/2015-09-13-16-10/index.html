Dear R,

I can imagine how you feel when you see these “Once”s in those titles. In fact, when I wrote them down, I thought I knew, since I had already tasted much your tears in these five years. I have to say I'm really sorry that these words hurt you.

But I didn’t want to blame you or hurt you for writing them down, they are just my feelings. I had to put them there, because I felt that I lost something. Some feelings that make me write down that titles.

You know, 钱钟书 once wrote some words for 杨绛:
      绝无仅有的结合了各不相容的三者：妻子，情人，朋友。

I think I had some similar feelings like this. I guess you can see the similarity between these three words and your titles. But after I knew what you did, these feelings had gone. How could I still claim these titiles in the previous letter as nothing happened, even though I knew the these “Once”s will break your heart?

In your letter, you said I am just like a family to you now. But I am sorry to say, maybe I am like a family of yours, but it’s impossible to be true if we define the real families are bonded by blood. Maybe I would be your husband in the future, which perhaps can be called a true family, especially if we have some children. But you shall know, I think we are bonded by some other things, some other feelings that I prefer use other words than family.

So, this means that we are NOT bonded eternally. For families, they are bonded by blood, which never can be broken, even by death. Do you remember the Unbreakable Vow? Do you find in fact it can and only can be broken by death? What a pity, and what a luck, to say love is not an Unbreakable Vow. In fact, how fragile the love is, how easy to break it into pieces, though it's maybe crafted by so many days and nights.

Therefore, maybe someone will leave away from other one, when our 羁绊 breaks in pieces.

On the other hand, what the exact feeling to be a family? If you like to read stories on 知乎, you will find so many sad stories happened in families, so many people wanted to run away from this eternal bond. In such cases, the feeling of family was terrible.

Of course, I knew that you meant some good feeling. But what exact feeling was that? In your letter of 19, July, you wrote, your parents would never get interested in the books you read. So I can guess your feeling to me was not like your feelings to your parents, since we shared so many things. And in fact, I couldn't attach myself to any position in your family relations. Brother? Yes, in some sense. But I think it was far more than that in some other sense. In fact, I think our bond was much more beyond any family. As I wrote in the previous letter, once, we were just like siamese twins: we shared all most everything in the everyday life and we spent all most all the time together.

But please don’t say that I was like a brother who can help you and share things with you. I was always there to help you and always wanted to share everything with you. But I did those things just because they made me happy. I still remember that several years ago, when sometimes (maybe most times) I went alone to the lectures on history of western literatures, I always found so many things that made me wish that you were just be my side. So we could share them together. Only that desire to share those things with you is enough to make me happy at that times. And we did share many things in these five years, which always brought happiness to me.

On the other hand, I never thought I could do something which only your true brother and family can do. That is, if my sister has a boyfriend, even a husband, and if she goes to travel with some other man without telling her boyfriend, if she writes letters in three months without letting her boyfriend know, I won’t blame her, even it’s not good in my opinion. I will just tell her, follow your heart, to find your true love.

But what a luck and what a pity, I am not your brother. And so I can’t feel nothing for what you had done, I can’t stop to fall in sorrows, since I am not your brother, but perhaps love you more than a brother in some senses.

Anyway, I’m grateful to know that you said I had brought many new possibilities to your life, that you like the things that I appreciate much, that you will treasure our sweet memories. And I’m also happy to see that you found that sometimes I was like a boy who knows nothing and needs your care. I think this is even true in all 26 years since I came to this world, though maybe you didn’t notice this very clearly in our early time. According to some post on 知乎, it maybe just means that I fell in love with you.

However, now we know, many many things had been broken, include yours and mines.

In your letter of 9, June, you said heartbreakings make us cherish what we have, and I think the loss does, too. I think we had lost many many things now, and I wish we will cherish them next time, if we could have them again.

And now you have seen it’s quite abnormal if someone just wants to be your brother without anymore desire. But on the other hand, to like you, to cherish you, to view you as very specious one to himself, to love you, are so normal, since you are so lovely. And much more for me, since we had been together for more than five years, and in which we went through so many things, happy and sad.

And you shall know, there is some other thing that is different between I and X. I believe I won’t ask some girl to be my girlfriend if I already know that she has a boyfriend. I still remembered that situation years ago when I asked you if you have a boyfriend. As I guessed, you said none. Not a surprise, but what a happiness to me at that moment. Now, you told X that you had a boyfriend at very beginning. But anyway, it seemed uselesss to stop him from asking you to be his girlfriend finally at 31 August. Maybe, you can learn something from this in the future. As I guess, something you can learn first is, you can’t hope others think as you hope. As you wanted X just to be your brother. What a mistake and a lesson you learn, maybe to your sorrows. And a second lesson I hope you can learn is, you shall say “NO” no matter what others say to ask you to be his girfriend when you already have a boyfriend. Even if you think this one is your true love. Of course, if you like this one much more, you always can break with the first one then try to be close with the other one. But saying that “NO”, is a manner, I guess Dumbledore will agree with me, since Dumbledore always has his manners even before his death.

However, though I believe I won’t do such a thing, I can understand it. Otherwise, we won’t like Princess Rhaegar or Jack Dawson. We won't be happy to see that Knox pursues Chris Noel after he is taught to “carpe diem, seize the day”.

But what do you hope me to react after all of this? I thought I can’t tolerate that my girlfriend won’t say no within a second. I thought I can’t believe my girlfriend will say she need to time to consider. But now I find I was wrong, I tolerated, and I waited. I guess you know why. But now I have to say, I can’t wait any longer. I’m sorry, le vent se lève, and winter has arrived. And I’m really sorry I break another promise to you.

But please feel free to make any decisions. For youself, it doesn’t matter if other ones’ words are moving, only the whisper of your heart does matter. Though maybe this whisper is covered by noise, hard to listen. And please to make decisions, to look forward to the remaining voyage of life, bravely, like Nausicaä; hopefully, like San; and even with great loss, even when le vent se lève.

Your once boyfriend,
S
